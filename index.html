Bible Adventure!

🌟 Old Testament 🌟

  • 🌟 God created everything in 6 days – shows His power as Creator
  • 👤 Humans created on Day 6 – special creation in God’s image
  • 👫 Adam & Eve – first humans
  • 🌳 Forbidden: Tree of Knowledge – test of obedience
  • 🐍 Sin entered through serpent deception – beginning of human sin
🔑 Key Points: Creation → Fall → Sin → Separation
Q: Who tempted Eve?
A: 🐍 Serpent
  • 🚢 Noah built the ark – saved from God’s judgment
  • 🌧️ Rain: 40 days – worldwide flood
  • 🌈 Sign of covenant: Rainbow – God’s promise
  • 🏗️ Babel: languages confused – stopped human pride
🧠 Mnemonic: F.R.A.M.E → Flood, Rain, Ark, Many languages, Earth repopulated
Q: What bird did Noah send first?
A: 🐦 Raven
  • ⭐ Abraham – father of faith, trusted God
  • 👶 Son promised: Isaac – fulfillment of God’s promise
  • 🔁 Jacob → Israel – renamed by God
  • 👥 12 Tribes formed – nation of Israel begins
  • 👑 Joseph – dreams, slavery, became ruler in Egypt
🔄 Pattern: Promise → Testing → Fulfillment
Q: Who sold Joseph?
A: 👥 His brothers
  • 👨‍🦯 Moses led Israel – chosen leader
  • ⚡ 10 Plagues – God judged Egypt
  • 🌊 Crossed Red Sea – miracle escape
  • 🍞 Manna provided – daily food from God
  • 💧 Water from rock – God’s provision
🧠 Mnemonic: P.R.O.M.I.S.E (Plagues, Red Sea, Obedience, Manna, Instructions, Sinai, Escape)
Q: Where were commandments given?
A: ⛰️ Mount Sinai
  • 👑 First king: Saul – beginning of monarchy
  • 🪨 David defeated Goliath – faith in God
  • 🧠 Solomon – wisdom & temple builder
  • ⚔️ Kingdom divided after Solomon – Israel split
Q: What did Solomon ask?
A: 🧠 Wisdom
  • 🔥 Elijah vs Baal – proved true God
  • ✨ Elisha miracles – continued Elijah’s work
  • 🦁 Daniel – lions’ den (faithfulness)
  • 🐟 Jonah – sent to Nineveh (repentance)
📚 Major Prophets: Isaiah, Jeremiah, Ezekiel, Daniel
Q: Who was swallowed by fish?
A: 🐟 Jonah
  • ➡️ Creation → Fall → Flood → Patriarchs → Exodus → Kings → Prophets
  • 🎯 Focus: God’s covenant, law, and chosen people Israel
